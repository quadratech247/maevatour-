California Dreamin’ —
Your Ultimate Golden State Adventure

Welcome to the land where the sun kisses the Pacific and dreams become reality. Our California Experience travel package is your golden ticket to adventure, culture, and relaxation – all in one unforgettable week.

1

Welcome to Los Angeles

Land in the City of Angels. Get starstruck on Hollywood Boulevard, stroll through Santa Monica, and watch the sunset at Venice Beach. Optional: Studio tour at Warner Bros or Universal Studios.

HollywoodVenice BeachLA Arrival
2

The Glam and Glitz of LA

Hike the Hollywood Hills for panoramic views. Explore the Getty Center’s world-class art, then end your day with fine dining in Beverly Hills.

Hollywood Hills HikeGetty CenterBeverly Hills
3

Pacific Coast Highway Road Trip

Travel the scenic Highway 1 to Santa Barbara. Stop at Malibu’s iconic beaches. Enjoy wine tasting in Santa Ynez Valley and an overnight stay in charming coastal accommodations.

Malibu BeachSanta BarbaraWine Tasting
4

San Francisco via Big Sur

Drive through Big Sur’s cliffs and redwoods. Arrive in San Francisco – explore the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, and Fisherman’s Wharf. Ride the famous cable cars and enjoy a sunset cruise in the bay.

Big Sur CliffsGolden Gate BridgeSunset Cruise
6

Wine Country Wonders

Venture to Napa and Sonoma. Savor tastings at award-winning wineries, indulge in gourmet farm-to-table cuisine, and relax in vineyard-side spas.

Napa ValleySonoma SpasGourmet Dining
7

Farewell California

Brunch in Sausalito with views of the Bay, then transfer to the airport. You’ll leave with full hearts, stunning photos, and golden memories.
